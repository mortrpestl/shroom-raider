# Yield After Return

_Submission #1 for the Level Design Project_


Created By: **Diogn Mortera**

## Motivations 

This level was designed to:
- Introduce you to mechanics that you probably took for granted before, like:
    - rocks not being able to pass through items (so you have to remove the items from the path)
    - forced sequences of events and certain tree-breaking positions to progress through the level
    - and more! play the level :D

- Be difficult and only moderately long in a fair way. 
- Look beautiful! from bird's eye view, it looks like a smiley face with question-mark eyes. Isn't it cute?

The map is titled "Yield After Return" as a play on generators having to be _as lazy as possible_, just like most item-collecting sequences and rock sequences in this map.

Do enjoy! The solution video is at the bottom.
